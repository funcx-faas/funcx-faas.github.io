funcXFest - The funcX Community Meeting

The annual funcXFest community meeting brings together researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts from around the world to discuss experiences using and developing funcX and to learn more about funcX.

Parsl & funcX Fest 2022

The second funcXFest community meeting will be held as a free hybrid meeting on September 13-14. The meeting will include presentations from researchers, developers, and cyberinfrastructure experts as well as the funcX team.

Parsl & funcX Fest 2021

The first funcXFest community meeting was held as a free virtual meeting over two sessions (Oct 27 morning and Oct 28 afternoon). The meeting included 29 presentations from funcX and Parsl users and developers.
